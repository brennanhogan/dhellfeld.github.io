About me

I recently obtained a master's degree in nuclear engineering from Texas A&M University with a specialization in nuclear nonproliferation through the Nuclear Security Science and Policy Institute. I am currently attending the University of California, Berkeley working towards a PhD in nuclear engineering and hold a fellowship from the Nuclear Science and Security Consortium to conduct research in collaboration with Lawrence Livermore National Laboratory. My research interests include novel radiation detection methods for nuclear security and safeguards applications.
